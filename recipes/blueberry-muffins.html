Blueberry Oat Muffins

A delicious blueberry muffin topped with streusel

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 largs eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • Cooking spray

For streusel:

  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 6 tablespoons butter

Steps

Muffin:

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Combine oats and buttermilk in a medium mixing bowl. Let sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Add oil, eggs, and sugar to oat mixture; beat for 1 minute.
  4. Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a seperate bowl.
  5. Add flour mixture to oat mixture and stir until combined.
  6. Fold berries into the batter.
  7. Spoon batter into muffin cups (coated with cooking spray) to 3/4 full.

Streusel:

  1. In a small bowl, combine flour, sugar and oats.
  2. Mix in butter with fingers or a pastry cutter until mixture resembles coarse meal.
  3. Sprinkle mixture over batter.
  4. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
